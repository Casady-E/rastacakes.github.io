Case Studies

Connecting floral stores with customers around the world.

Bloom makes flower deliveries easy. By connecting local and nationwide floral services in one place, Bloom delivers great bouquets and arrangements at the best available price. Bloom parterned with Pau Street Studio to develop their corporate identity and design their new app interface to make it more user centric and connect with their audience.

Uniting the fencing community.

En Garde promotes local fencing communities and competitions through a unique challenge interface while also providing engaging content, live fixtures, and events. En Garde partnered with Pau Street Studio to design their corporate identity and create their entire app interface that includes their proprietary fencing battle platform.

Worldwide commodities and investment trading.

Mercari Global trades high quantity & quality commodities or investments with global clients. They have a reputation of pushing the boundaries of traditional logistics to expediate every delivery. Mercari Global partnered with Pau Street Studio for a complete rebranding of their corporate identity that coincided with their company values.

Linking the world through cutting edge communication technology.

Mondial Technology delivers, implements, and uninstalls telecommunications equipment through the entire lifecycle of the product. They partner with local governements and international companies to set up networks in even the most remote areas of the world. Mondial partnered with Pau Street Studio for a new corporate identity that reflected their business values and resonated with their audience.
